Bio

I studied chemistry in the university and did 8 years of research as a master and phd student on ultrafast spectroscopy. This field is a multi-disciplinary field where physics, electronics, optics, chemistry and even biology coincide. It was a good opportunity to learn a lot about fundamental science. There is more information on my research background in here.

In 2018, I moved into private sector as a software engineer. This was a dream of mine, and it turned into a reality. I work at Infor since 2018, which is one of the top ERP software company in the world. I am involved in development and maintanance of Infor LN Finance product.

I am happily working as a software engineer and I have a motivation to become better at it. For this reason, in the begining of 2020, I decided to learn the fundamentals of computer science & programming. Here I would like to touch on courses that I followed or still following.
